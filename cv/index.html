Career and Education

2023 - Present
  • Research Associate at School of Mathematical and Computer Sciences, Heriot-Watt University, Edinburgh, UK;
  • working on several EPSRC-funded projects about mathematical theory of polycrystalline materials with Prof. John Ball, Dr David Bourne, Dr Lucia Scardia.
2022 - 2023
  • Research Fellow at School of Mathematics, University of Birmingham, UK;
  • working on an EPSRC-funded project about analysis of evolutionary PDEs as gradient flows arising from Optimal Transportation Theory with Dr Hong Duong;
  • organising the Reading Group on Mathematics of Data Science (see Events).
2019 - 2022
  • Research Associate at School of Mathematics, Cardiff University, UK;
  • working on an EPSRC-funded project about stochastic elasticity with Dr Angela Mihai;
  • organising the Applied and Computational Mathematics Seminar in 2020/2021 and 2021/2022 (see YouTube channel).
2016 - 2019
  • PhD in Mathematics and Statistics at University of Warwick, UK;
  • funded by EPSRC as part of MASDOC Doctoral Training Centre;
  • Thesis: Atomistic modelling of fracture ;
  • Advisors: Prof. Christoph Ortner and Dr Thomas Hudson.
2015 - 2016
  • MSc in Mathematics and Statistics (Distinction) at University of Warwick, UK;
  • funded by EPSRC as part of MASDOC Doctoral Training Centre;
  • Thesis: Atomistic models for crystal defects;
  • Advisor: Prof. Christoph Ortner.
2011 - 2015
  • BSc with Honours in Mathematics (First class) at University of Strathclyde, UK;
  • Prizes: Gary Roach Prize, Sir Hermann Bondi Prize and Kelvin Prize.
