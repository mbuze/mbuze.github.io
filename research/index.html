Research


My research spans a wide range of topics at the intersection of applied and computational mathematics and mathematical analysis and is primarily inspired by applications in materials science, biology, physics and data science and includes an industrial collaboration with Tata Steel.



I currently have three major ongoing research projects which you read about by clicking the links:

  • Discrete modelling of nucleation and migration of defects in materials

  • Optimal transport - theory and applications

  • Uncertainty quantification for machine learning interatomic potentials
